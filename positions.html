Positions on The Court

Setters

A setter sets up the hitters for them to attack the ball. They are positioned in the back row, left side

Hitters

Outside Hitters

A front row hitter that hits on the left side of the court.

Middle Blockers

A front row hitter positioned in the middle of the court.

Opposite Hitters

A front row hitter on the right side of the court.

Backrow Players

  • Liberos
  • Defensive Specialists

Liberos and Defensive Specialists(DS) are backrow players that specialize in passing. They usually do not hit.
A difference between a libero and a defensive specialist is that a libero wears a different color jersey
than the rest of team. Liberos also do not have to do subsitutions, but defensive specialists do.
